# 📦 Amazon Product Detail Page API: Deep Intelligence Layer

The **Amazon Product Detail Page API** provides comprehensive, structured data for individual products across all Amazon regional marketplaces. This module is essential for businesses conducting product competitiveness analysis, user review mining, and pricing strategy reference.

## 🚀 Key Features

* **Core Product Information:** Extract high-fidelity data including Title, Link, ASIN, Price, and Brand information.
* **Real-Time Pricing & Inventory:** Monitor live prices and inventory status across different regional domains.
* **Product Variations:** Full support for variation information such as color, size, and other product-specific attributes.
* **Rich Content:** Access product descriptions, technical specifications, and categorized lists of advertised products.
* **Advanced Metrics:** Retrieve sales rankings, percentage distribution of star ratings, and comprehensive user reviews (username, rating, content, and time).
