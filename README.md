### 🚀 Amazon Extraction Capabilities:

#### 1. 🏷️ Product Listing & Catalog Data
* **Comprehensive Metadata:** Automated extraction of product titles, ASINs, technical specs, and category hierarchies.
* **Variant Intelligence:** Captures granular SKU-level data including size, color, and specific variant pricing.
* **Media Assets:** High-resolution extraction of product gallery images and video URLs for catalog automation.

#### 2. 💰 Real-Time Pricing & Buy Box Intelligence
* **Dynamic Pricing:** Monitoring of current prices, list prices, and discount percentages to protect margins.
* **Buy Box Status:** Real-time tracking of Buy Box winners to fuel competitive repricing strategies.
* **Deal Monitoring:** Automated alerts for Lightning Deals, Coupons, and "Best Deal" tags.

#### 3. 📊 Inventory & Seller Insights
* **Stock Levels:** Estimated inventory counts and availability status (In-Stock/Out-of-Stock).
* **Seller Intelligence:** Extraction of 3P seller names, ratings, and business profiles for market analysis.
* **Fulfillment Tracking:** Identifies fulfillment methods (Sold by Amazon vs. FBA vs. FBM).

#### 4. ⭐ Review & Sentiment Analysis
* **Advanced Review Scraper:** Captures review titles, star ratings, and "Verified Purchase" status.
* **Sentiment Intelligence:** Structured data feeds designed to analyze customer pain points and product trends.
* **QA Extraction:** Scrapes "Customer Questions & Answers" for deep-dive market research.
