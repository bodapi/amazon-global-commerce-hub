# 📦 Amazon Global Commerce: Full-Spectrum Data Intelligence Hub

Welcome to the ultimate Amazon data extraction repository. This hub provides comprehensive documentation and technical solutions for scraping high-fidelity data from Amazon's global marketplaces. Designed for market researchers, e-commerce brands, and price monitoring platforms.

### 🚀 Amazon Extraction Capabilities:

#### 1. 🏷️ Product Listing & Catalog Data
* **Comprehensive Metadata:** Extracts product titles, ASINs, descriptions, and category hierarchies.
* **Variant Tracking:** Captures all variations including size, color, and technical specifications.
* **Image & Media Assets:** High-resolution extraction of product gallery images and video links.

#### 2. 💰 Real-Time Pricing & Buy Box Intelligence
* **Dynamic Pricing:** Real-time monitoring of current prices, list prices, and discount percentages.
* **Buy Box Status:** Tracks which seller currently holds the Buy Box for competitive analysis.
* **Deal Monitoring:** Automated alerts for Lightning Deals, Coupons, and "Best Deal" tags.

#### 3. 📊 Inventory & Seller Insights
* **Stock Levels:** Estimated inventory counts and availability status (In-Stock, Out-of-Stock).
* **Seller Details:** Extraction of third-party seller names, ratings, and business addresses.
* **Fulfillment Tracking:** Identifies whether a product is "Sold by Amazon" or "Fulfilled by Amazon" (FBA).

#### 4. ⭐ Review & Sentiment Analysis
* **Full Review Scraper:** Captures review titles, body text, star ratings, and "Verified Purchase" status.
* **Review Sentiment:** Structured data feeds for analyzing customer pain points and satisfaction trends.
* **QA Extraction:** Scrapes the "Customer Questions & Answers" section for product research.

### 🛠️ Technical Advantage
- **Regional Support:** Optimized for all Amazon domains (US, UK, DE, JP, CN, etc.).
- **Anti-Bot Navigation:** Advanced rotation and proxy management to ensure continuous extraction without bans.
- **Structured JSON/CSV Output:** Ready-to-use data formats for rapid database integration.
