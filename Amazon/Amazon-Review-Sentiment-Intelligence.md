# ⭐ Amazon Customer Sentiment & Review Intelligence

Extract and analyze the "Voice of the Customer" to drive product innovation and brand protection. This module provides structured access to millions of data points within the Amazon feedback ecosystem.

### 🔍 Intelligence Capabilities:

* **Full Review Scraper:** Captures review titles, body text, star ratings, and "Verified Purchase" status across all global Amazon domains.
* **Media Asset Extraction:** High-resolution links to customer-uploaded photos and videos for visual quality audits.
* **Sentiment Identification:** Automatically categorizes feedback into positive, negative, or neutral streams for rapid response.
* **Q&A Monitoring:** Scrapes the "Customer Questions & Answers" section to identify market gaps and consumer pain points.
