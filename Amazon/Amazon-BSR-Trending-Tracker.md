# 📈 Amazon BSR & High-Velocity Trending Analytics

In the hyper-competitive Amazon marketplace, speed is everything. Our Trending Analytics module is engineered to track market shifts in real-time, allowing brands and resellers to capitalize on emerging trends before they hit the mainstream.

### 🚀 Advanced Tracking Capabilities:

#### 1. 🏆 Real-Time BSR Monitoring
* **Deep-Category Tracking:** Monitor Best Sellers Rank (BSR) across primary categories and niche sub-categories.
* **Rank Fluctuation Alerts:** Detect sudden jumps in ranking that signify a product is "going viral" or gaining massive traction.
* **Historical Ranking Data:** Access historical BSR charts to differentiate between long-term winners and short-term "fads."

#### 2. 🔥 Movers & Shakers Intelligence
* **Growth Velocity:** Identify products with the highest percentage gain in sales rank over the last 24 hours.
* **Trending Heatmaps:** Visualize which niches (e.g., Electronics, Home & Kitchen, Fashion) are currently seeing the most consumer activity.
* **New Release Tracking:** Automatically scrape and monitor the "Hot New Releases" section to spot upcoming market leaders.

#### 3. 📊 Sales Estimation & Volume Analysis
* **Sales-to-Rank Mapping:** Utilize proprietary algorithms to estimate monthly unit sales based on current BSR and category competition.
* **Top 100 Leaderboard:** Full extraction of the Top 100 Best Sellers in any global Amazon domain (US, UK, DE, etc.).
* **Out-of-Stock Forecasting:** Analyze sales velocity to predict when competitors are likely to run out of inventory.

#### 4. 🎯 Opportunity Identification
* **Low Competition Gaps:** Discover high-ranking products with low review counts—the "gold mine" for new market entrants.
* **Seasonal Trend Analysis:** Track year-over-year performance for seasonal products to optimize inventory for Q4 or holiday peaks.
