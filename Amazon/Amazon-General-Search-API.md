# 🔍 Amazon General Search API: Market Intelligence Layer

The **Amazon General Search API** is a high-fidelity data bridge engineered to provide real-time visibility into the Amazon Search Engine Results Page (SERP). It enables brands and market researchers to monitor rankings, competitive positioning, and sponsored product visibility across all global marketplaces.

## 🚀 Key Features

* **Organic vs. Sponsored Analysis:** Accurately distinguish between paid placements and natural search results to calculate Share of Voice (SOV).
* **Global SERP Localization:** Access search results exactly as a local customer would in the US, UK, EU (France), or LATAM (Brazil).
* **Rating & Review Volume Tracking:** Monitor social proof dynamics and consumer sentiment in real-time.
* **Infrastructure Resilience:** Built-in JS rendering and proxy rotation to bypass the most advanced anti-bot systems.
